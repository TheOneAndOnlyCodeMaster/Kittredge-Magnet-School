Ansley Floyd

Ms. Floyd

Ms. Floyd is the math teacher for the sixth grade. Each grade is divided into to groups, and for the sixth grade, there are comets and quasars. Ms. Floyd is on the quasars side. She is also my homeroom teacher. She's humorous, fun, and always tries to find fun ways to teach us math without losing our attention. She's the first class I have, and it's a great way to start the day!

Ms.Doss

Ms.Doss

Ms.Doss is my science teacher, and she is my secnd class of the day. She gave us a journal to write down our science notes, and with virtual learning, she'e making a slide show so if we forget about anything, we an refer to it for information. She's really flexible and gives us very intersting stroies and facts, usually about the subject we're studying, and she somtimes even let's us leave the zoom call early to relax before the next class

Ms.Dunlap

Ms.Dunlap

Ms.Dunlap is my Social Studies teacher. Regularly, Every day she starts off class with riddle to warm up our minds, and she is my 4th class. She tries to make everything fun and as short as possible so we can enjoy our day. We always finish with a competitive quiz and she lets us ask questions at the end. She's been all around the world working, and she tells us all about her experiences.

Ms.Wilkins

Ms.Wilkins

Ms.Wilkins is a great teacher and always tries to keep us smiling. She uses funny terms and let's us be a little more free. She is my 5th class of the day and she teaches us English Language Arts (ELA) She is the tallest teacher at our school. In her class, we read pages and chapters of our current book and she even gave us a "mentor book" which has all the information for the school year.
